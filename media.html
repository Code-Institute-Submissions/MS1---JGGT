This first track is from my band When Our Time Comes' debut self titled album. It's more of a rhythmically challenging song, particularly the bridge section. The bridge starts off with a clean guitar part arpeggiating the chorus chords before kicking into a heavy alternate picked 16th note riff that bounces back and forth between 4/4 and 3/4 time every bar. At a tempo of 148bpm it can be a very fatiguing passage for the right hand to play and required a lot of metronome practice to get it sounding tight!
This track is another When Our Time Comes song, this time from our debut EP. This again comes with some more rhythmically challenging aspects with most of the song being in 7/8 time as well as some big chord shapes in the chorus. The solo features some advanced techniques such as two handed tapping and some fast legato runs over more odd time signatures.
